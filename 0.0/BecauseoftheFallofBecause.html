Life of Em: A Drawing from a Autistic Youngling to a Acoustic, Practicing Shaman

Em once confided to me that a long time ago, as he was in the midst of his manipura chakra and in his late teenage years that he wanted to draw. Being a codependent teenager with as much self esteem as a starving student has money, Em ofcourse wanted to draw himself as a buff, cool dude - maybe some long hair and then to spellcast the whole experience to hell and back as a rock star, right? Why not, ey? [The teenage angst Em makes the grown up dito cringe - just a tad bit…]

So, some anatomy by a shy, autistic, single and indeed frustrated teenager. What could possibly go wrong...

Well, the said rockstar had apparently become drawn skin deep and in the flesh from the waist up, and of course then - since feelings and faces were a problematic concept - with his head tilted back and all the long hair over what remained of the facial features when this mythical rock star was drawn from the front.

“But since the whole point was to draw anatomy - why bother with the face, right?” said Em, and continued explaining that the body was the focal point of this doodle, and what felt the most interesting in this creation of his was the belly and the throat. Hence the tilted back head and stretched out arms was kind of a given.

“But, wait…?”, I said in a try to warm up the mirrors and for Em, I tried to explain a thing remembered with a shaman practicing in front of his herd naked - showing his genitals, bowels and throat and at the same time spreading out the arms in a sign to his near and dear - “I’m safe here, and I help to heal and lead you!”, a shaman would indeed feel empowered by the presence of his family, channeling their trust to tell and sing stories.

“And is it not so”, reflected Em, “that telling your own story would be therapeutic?”

“It seems that so it is indeed said”, I retorted - feeling all grand in the mirroring.

This all made me remember a certain day a month or so prior where - after having literally been walking in circles for hours I landed in front of a church. Sitting in front of the church and in awe of its architecture I had the strong feeling that my calling was contained within its ponderous presence, and so I went in to have myself a phoney, grounded conversation. Finding nothing much in the hall of the church, I had gone in to have a seat and things started flooding in from the underworld. One of the things manifesting itself was an animation of a person in the same posture as Em’s rockstar, and then fast forwarding to another shamans posture - Jesus on the cross. So it was said that the Romans took the discontending leaders of the time and locked their hands and feet with nine inch nails so that they could neither wander nor practice their healing powers, and the top part of the pole made the shamans not being able to star sing, to stretch their throats proper - practicing their power by mimicking the sounds of animals and nature.

Life of Em[11]: 513 days in The Boat with an Ogre and His Henchman

Intro

In the very beginning of 2018 a friend that just recently has been diagnosed with ADHD tells Em “I see a lot of me in you” and so he starts to seek information and look up symptoms online and later professional attention. This has Em bouncing around in the psychology system in Tokholm for about 8-9 months with no actual progress, diagnosis or medication.

This year and the previous has also seen Em drinking more both in volume, in frequency - with others and also more often in his own solitude.

Ironically enough this same period also sees Em training more and more rigorously, and he also invests in and starts renovating an apartment - a long longed for dream.

In this stressful time of his life Em not only loses his job, but also spends more time doing nothing in a mist of more manic depressive behaviour to feed up an empty self.

The ADHD-evaluation process in Tokholm needs to be aborted however, since Em has finally found a company in Perlin that wants him as not only as a developer but also as a team lead and a senior position, to boot!

So, after almost a lifetime of yearning to move abroad Em finally has found it within himself to leave Tokholm and seek adventure in Perlin - The City of Randomized Life and Noise!

After a while of searching and struggles he found a short lasting romance with a company called HiveLog, a small startupish fully owned by The Boat! After a very short employment Em sadly gets the news that the company is to be shut down at the end of year and so, saddened by this news, restarts with interviews and hiring processes all over again. In the process of shutting HiveLog down, the mothership tries to offer the HiveLog crew different positions in The Boat and so a certain Happiness Manager(tm) has a very forceful and serious sounding interview with Em over the phone, together with two seemingly random attendees, all this to - Em guesses - vet Em, at least somehow.This interview was also held over the phone, although Em is still in Perlin...

At the end of December Em moves back to Tokholm for a month over the holiday season to assess different possibilities, make his own flat ready for subletting and of course celebrate with friends and family. He however gladly finally accepts a new offer from The Boat in a project working with Eccentric Cycles at one of their finest offices in Perlin - all under this mysterious Manager of Happiness!

Finding a Position in the Boat and Setting Sail

So, restarting in January of 2019 he gets to work under two certain individuals - the coy, lanky, happiness managing ogre Buli Coerzer and his underling Coynards Schlechtytown! In a stressful time Em moves apartments 3 times, and attends so many apartment viewings he loses count. He manages at least somewhat to find a foothold in The Boat with both his professional and social situations being at least somewhat stable. The finicky Ogre however checks in on Em through a 1:1 - this first of many being very loosey-goosey and unspecified… And also the actual, booked time slot is on a Saturday?

Sadly, already during his probationary period Buli raises a complaint about Em’s subpar performance and so Em confides with Buli that he previously sought professional attention regarding ADHD. Buli - instead of firing this extremely troublesome, rebellious and subpar employee during the probationary period as is lawful in the country of Perlin - emails Em some haphazard information related to ADHD…

[190423 - Loads of information]

[meeting before GFB/WGT vacation]

In this third 1:1 - shortly before vacation -  Em is given a homework assignment! Since Buli can’t seem to use a search engine anymore Em should compile a list of information regarding ADHD!

Buli speaks about himself having problems with maladroicy regarding his responsibilities at work since he is regretful about not having fired Em during his probationary period, but confides in Em that he has seen these problems regarding Em’s for as long as they have been working together. To Em they look like the standard ADHD and depression bullet point list - late arrivals, uncommunicative, late to meetings, not productive enough, slow to finalize tasks…

Em trusts Buli and so finds it within himself to finally speak up and be honest about his lifelong problems with mental health issues and his family, and maybe if Buli could help him in this time of need? See, finding a therapist in Perlin when one does not speak local tongue properly had so far turned out to be a struggle! Buli promises Em to contact HR and see what they can do for Em and so the bond of trust Em feels towards Buli and the Boat starts to grow stronger...

[190627 - Just before vacation]

After a couple of weeks - in the days just before Ems vacation - during a so suddenly booked 1:1 it is decided (on Em’s initiative) that there will be a team meeting about Ems situation and that Em should do some homework during vacation to find resources for Buli online in hopes that Buli can study ADHD himself. Em so wonders if it is so that Buli is so inept at his work or technology in general that he cannot even enter the frase “ADHD” into a search engine like he seemingly could before, but abides anywho!

Perhaps everyone in the team was given homework assignments about mental health issues?

Em today finds it funny that this “homework” was OK to send over Slack but that list with local psychotherapists that takes public insurance was given to Buli on a USB-stick… This list with concrete information seems somehow to be of less importance… Can it be so that it was beneficial with plasticky sticks that leave no papery trails in all of this? Who knows with coy ogres, maybe one more email is one too many, after all!

20190628 - Big team meeting

During this hastily scheduled team meeting on the 28th of June - just before Em’s second summer vacation - where the invitation puts all responsibilities on Em and there is no mentioning or references to mental health issues whatsoever - a lot of confused team members join in, hearing how Buli sees his point of view and then how he suddenly has an excuse to up and leave when it is time for Em to speak.

Em gets a bit nonplussed by this behaviour - Surely it would be in the best interest of Buli to understand Ems point of view? And also, would not a presence of the workers council or a union be advised at this kind of gathering? Surely it would have been informing for Em and his coworkers to know about their rights and possibilities regarding discrimination in general and mental health issues specifically in this case - in fact any kind of presence from a knowledgeable and neutral part would have been a bonus for everyone involved, Em today stipulates.

Em confusedly starts talking about his struggles and what objective information he can come up with from the top of his head, thinking Buli would have prepared a more proper presentation based on the material in the homework but does as good as he can, anywho! All the while Coynards silently sinks down behind his laptop when Em starts to speak, as if he was seeking protection...

After the meeting Em gets a lot of support, understanding and tips from his teammates and feels at least somewhat proud of himself - it’s a good feeling, that of speaking up with and about your issues - especially such that are so stigmatized that they can be used for discrimination!

...and just as so, the position for Em feels snugger in the Boat, and the net of trust is more strongly woven! - Em has even been moved to a deserted part of the office, where it is not only calm and quiet, but he is separated from the team and where him and Buli can talk undisturbed...

The Season of Change

During late summer and autumn Em plods further up the stream of depression, the drinking worsen and the solitude in Perlin is having an effect on his morale. He feels both guilt about leaving all of his friends and family in Tokholm, grief knowing how different everything could have been had he just known about his mental health issues earlier, and even a bit of empty nest syndrome because of the fact that he adopted his loved cat away when moving abroad.

He is so desperate for social stimuli outside of work he one day posts in an online forum to hang out with people which luckily gives at least some result!

Meanwhile at The Boat the first stop during this period is finally a booking for a therapist! Buli comes up to Em’s solicited isolation and makes extra sure the verbal information is super-duper clear… And then goes to his computer downstairs and sends Em the same information in email form - this information is indeed good to have a papertrail of, especially since the therapist linked to in the email has never worked at the company where Em is supposed to go and is a one man office - He does seem to have his office in the same suburb, though...

After the meeting it was perhaps realized we needed some paper-trails after all!

A fictional booking or a booked fiction? Who knows?!

This discrepancy between verbal and written communication - Em today wonders if that is not called gaslighting? Or is behavioural flatulence common with maladroit ogres? Em ponders and wonders, maybe the subject of maladroicy in authoritarian ogres needs to be studied further? For the benefit of all professional software constructing keyboard touchers, of course!
